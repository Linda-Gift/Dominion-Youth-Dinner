Rules of Engagement

Welcome to "More Than a Royal Banquet"! To ensure a regal and memorable experience for all, please adhere to the following royal decrees. Your cooperation helps maintain the elegance and smooth flow of our event.

  1. Maintain a respectful and courteous demeanor towards all guests, staff and the church.
  2. Adhere to Schedule: Be on time and follow the event's timings.
  3. Kindly follow directions from event staff and volunteers; they are here to assist you.
  4. No Smoking/Vaping: No smoking or vaping anywhere on church grounds.
  5. No Alcohol/Intoxicants: No alcohol or drugs allowed on church premises.
  6. Photography is welcome for personal memories, but please avoid obstructing views or using disruptive flashes during key moments.
  7. Personal Belongings: Keep your items safe; the church isn't responsible for lost items.
  8. Food & Beverages: Eat and drink only in designated areas; handle all utensils carefully to avoid spills.
  9. Award Items: Handle awards with care; follow stage directions if you are an awardee.
  10. Promotional Materials: Take only what you need from tables; don't litter.
  11. Decorations: Do not touch or move any decorations or church property.
  12. Waste Bins: Use the provided bins for all trash.
  13. Recycling: Separate recyclables if you can
  14. Utilize marked waste bins for all disposables and report any spills immediately to staff.
  15. Leave No Trace: Clear your area of personal items and trash before leaving.
  16. Restroom Etiquette: Keep restrooms clean and use waste bins provided.
